mahindra XUV 3XO

The Mahindra XUV300 is a compact SUV available with two engine options: Petrol Engine: 1.2-liter turbocharged unit producing 110 bhp and 200 Nm of torque. Diesel Engine: 1.5-liter turbocharged unit delivering 115 bhp and 300 Nm of torque. Both engines are paired with a 6-speed manual transmission, with an AMT option available for the diesel variant.

Rolls Royce Phantom

The Rolls-Royce Phantom is powered by a 6.75L twin-turbocharged V12 engine, producing 563 hp and 900 Nm of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission driving the rear wheels. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 5.3 seconds, offering a smooth yet powerful ride. Designed for ultimate luxury, it features a signature starlight headliner, bespoke handcrafted interiors, and an advanced air suspension system that ensures a whisper-quiet and effortlessly smooth driving experience.

Mercedes Benz E Class

The Mercedes-Benz E-Class comes with a range of engine options, including a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 producing up to 255 hp and 400 Nm of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission for smooth performance. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in around 6.1 seconds, depending on the variant. The E-Class offers a luxurious interior with premium materials, advanced MBUX infotainment, and driver-assist features. Its refined suspension system ensures a comfortable and dynamic driving experience.

BMW M4

The BMW M4 is powered by a 3.0L twin-turbocharged inline-6 engine, delivering up to 503 hp and 650 Nm of torque in the Competition variant. It comes with either a 6-speed manual or an 8-speed automatic transmission, with power sent to the rear wheels or BMW's xDrive all-wheel-drive system. The M4 accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in as little as 3.5 seconds, offering razor-sharp handling and track-ready performance.
